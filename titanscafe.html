Titan's Cafe

Titan's cafe is an online ordering system built for the Central Bucks South school cafe utilizing WordPress, WooCommerce, and custom PHP. As a leader of the Computer Programming Club, I led development for both the frontend and backend portions of the ordering system, which involved: setting up the content management system, creating custom PHP for the website, and organizing roles of the club members. I was also responsible for communicating with cafe management in order to make any necessary changes that the client requested. On top of that, I also captured and edited the necessary media for the user interface of the ordering system.
